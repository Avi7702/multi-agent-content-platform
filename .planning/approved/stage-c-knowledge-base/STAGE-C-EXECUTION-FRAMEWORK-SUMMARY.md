## ðŸ’¡ Key Insights from Research

### What Top AI Companies Do
1. **Start simple, iterate based on data** - Don't over-engineer upfront
2. **Automate everything possible** - CI/CD, monitoring, reporting
3. **Build in observability** - Real-time dashboards, metrics
4. **High trust, low ego** - Kind, direct communication
5. **Version control everything** - Code, prompts, configs, models

### What Works for 5-Week AI Projects
1. **Milestone-Kanban Hybrid** - Structure + flexibility
2. **Weekly OKRs** - Clear objectives, measurable results
3. **Daily 15-min standups** - Fast blocker resolution
4. **WIP limits** - Focus on completion, not starting
5. **Weekly retrospectives** - Continuous improvement

### What to Avoid
1. âŒ **Waterfall planning** - Too rigid for AI projects
2. âŒ **Pure Kanban** - Lacks structure for complex work
3. âŒ **No critical path** - Can't identify blockers early
4. âŒ **Manual tracking** - Too slow, error-prone
5. âŒ **Weekly-only updates** - Issues discovered too late
